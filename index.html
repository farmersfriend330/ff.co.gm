Welcome to Farmers Friend

Empowering Farmers, Building Climate Resilience

About Us

Farmers Friend is a social enterprise dedicated to supporting farmers in climate-vulnerable regions by raising awareness about climate change, advocating for sustainable farming practices, and providing resources to enhance food security and resilience.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in farmer advocacy and climate resilience, ensuring that farmers have the tools, knowledge, and support needed to thrive in a changing climate.

Our Mission

To support farmers in climate-vulnerable regions by raising awareness about climate change, advocating for sustainable farming practices, and providing resources to enhance food security and resilience.

Our Programs

  • Content Creation: Articles, journals, and reports on climate change and agriculture.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Social media campaigns, webinars, and workshops.
  • Farmer Support: Resources for sustainable farming practices.
  • Advocacy: Policy recommendations and partnerships with policymakers.

Our Impact

At Farmers Friend, we are transforming agriculture through education, advocacy, and farmer support.

Through our programs:

  • We have educated farmers and the public about the impacts of climate change on agriculture.
  • We have provided actionable resources and tools for sustainable farming practices.
  • We have advocated for policy changes that support farmers and promote climate resilience.
